What we do

Three disciplines. One record.

01

HDR Photography

Architectural photography shot in high dynamic range and processed to render the property the way the architect intended — not the way a camera sensor sees it by default. Natural light preserved. Every room documented.

02

Cinematic Tours

Property films shot on cinema glass with intentional pacing, colour grading, and original score. Not a walkthrough — a record of what it feels like to move through a well-made home.

03

Editorial AI Staging

Vacant rooms staged with editorial precision using AI — furniture, textiles, and objects chosen to complement the architecture, not obscure it. Delivered alongside the originals.

How we work

A process built around the architect, not the deadline.

01

Inquire

Tell us about the property. We'll review the listing and respond within 24 hours with availability and a preliminary scope.

02

Walk the property

Before production, we walk every room. We map the light, note the architecture's strongest angles, and plan the shoot around what the property actually offers.

03

Production

A focused production day — usually four to six hours. No crew of ten. No chaos. The property stays composed throughout.

04

Delivery

Edited stills and film within five business days. Full-resolution files, ready for MLS, print, and press. No watermarks on final delivery.

Studio

Vesta Folio is a property film and photography studio based in Los Angeles.

We work with agents and homeowners who understand that the record of a property outlasts the sale. The images we make document an address at a specific moment — the morning light through a particular window, the patina on a thirty-year-old floor, the garden at its peak. That moment does not repeat.

— The work serves the architecture. We never compete with it.

— Every property is photographed as if it will be published. Because it will.

— We keep the roster small so every client gets the full attention of the studio.
